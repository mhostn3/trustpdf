Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws in Portugal and the European Union.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences or previous activity.

What Cookies We Use

We use only Google Analytics 4 (GA4) cookies to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site. This helps us understand traffic patterns and improve the content and functionality of our website.

The data collected includes:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the website
  • Geographical region (general, not precise location)
  • Device and browser type

These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information and are used solely for statistical purposes.

Cookie List

The following cookies may be set by Google Analytics:

  • _ga: Used to distinguish users. Expiry: 2 years
  • _ga_XXXXXXX: Used to persist session state. Expiry: 2 years

Managing Your Preferences

We only set Google Analytics cookies if you provide your consent through our cookie banner. You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing cookies in your browser or adjusting the settings through our banner (when revisiting the site).

How to Disable Cookies

You can also disable cookies manually in your browser settings. Please refer to your browser's help section for instructions.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about the cookies used and the data collected
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data (although we do not collect any personally identifiable data)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Portugal, this is the Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados)

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how we use cookies, please contact us at: info@koplemo.com
